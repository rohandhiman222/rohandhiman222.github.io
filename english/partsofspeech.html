# Coordinating

A coordinating conjunction connects two words, phrases or clauses that are grammatically equal.

FANBOYS For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So

  • Ram and Rohan went upstairs.
  • I don't like pizza, burger and sandwish.
  • Ram must be in the room or on the roof
  • I don't like pizza, nor do i like burger.
  • I went to the shop, but it was close.
  • She was tired, yet she went there.
  • I was hungry, so i ate the apple.
  • We all were very tired, for we had stayed up all night.

Note :

  • They always come between the words or clauses they join.
